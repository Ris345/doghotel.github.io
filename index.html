About Us

We are a cozy hotel located in the heart of New York City. Over the years we had the vision to open a dog hotel. After years of planning and dedication we have managed to put up the world's finest dog hotel at one of the busiest cities in the world. We provide special treatment for our pet friends and also provide training for our young pet friends. We welcome every dog at this hotel.
